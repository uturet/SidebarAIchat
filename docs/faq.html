FAQ

Can I use multiple AI providers?
Yes. Add each provider in the settings and choose the one you need per chat.

Where are my chats stored?
Chats are saved locally in your browser. Clearing your cache will remove them.

Is there a cost to use SidebarAI Chat?
The extension is free. You only pay for usage from your API provider.
